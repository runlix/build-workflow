# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, constructive participation in issues, pull requests, reviews, and other project spaces.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in technical disagreement.
- Assume good intent and focus on the work.
- Give and receive feedback directly and constructively.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and personal attacks.
- Respect the privacy and safety of other contributors.

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, review comments, and any other project-managed communication channel.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to `conduct@runlix.io`.
Reports are handled privately.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this policy.
Responses can include warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent removal from project spaces, depending on severity and repetition.

## Attribution

This policy is informed by the Contributor Covenant project and adapted for this repository's maintenance model.
